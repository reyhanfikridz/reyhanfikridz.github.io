Bachelor's Thesis

My bachelor's thesis title in English is "Implementation of Neural Network to Recognize Person's Personality Using Big Five Personality Model Based On Video Game Genre Rating Given By Respondents". It is focused in Machine Learning.

Big five personality model, like the name says, has five personality dimension that is openness to experience, conscientiousness, extraversion, agreeableness, and neuroticism. This thesis/project/research produced best results at conscientiousness personality dimension with validation RMSE value of 0.79459.
